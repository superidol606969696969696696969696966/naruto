Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki is a young ninja who bears a great power hidden inside him, a power that has isolated him from the rest of his village. Shunned because of the power of the Nine-Tailed Fox spirit sealed within him, Naruto is shuthe leader of his village, and gain the respect of the other villagers. He trains hard, makes new friends, and works diligently to achieve his dream, despite the hardships he faces along the way.
